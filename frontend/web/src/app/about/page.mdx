# About the Founder

Master Within was founded by **Souvik Ghosh, PhD**, a researcher, author, and practitioner of contemplative traditions and philosophy.

---

## The Vision

The foundation grew out of a simple observation: modern education and technology focus heavily on external skills, leaving the human internal dimension largely untended. 

Souvik's research spans cognitive science, classical metaphysics, and the science of habits, aiming to synthesize academic rigor with practical spiritual exercises.

---

## Academic & Contemplative Path

Souvik completed his doctoral research in cognitive studies, analyzing the relationship between focused awareness and creative synthesis. His work has since focused on deconstructing cognitive bias and researching systems of peace.

Through books, structured learning pathways, and the Wisdom Library publication, his goal is to establish Master Within as a permanent digital sanctuary for rigorous and contemplative life inquiries.

> "To understand the universe, one must first study the mirror."

---

## Contact & Inquiry

Souvik actively guides the curriculum design and writes the weekly Substack publication essays. If you are an academic, practitioner, or seeker interested in collaborating on research or curriculum development, please feel free to reach out via the [Contact Page](/contact).
